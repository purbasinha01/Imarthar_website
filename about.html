Our Mission: Why Imarthar.Core Exists

Imarthar.Core began as a dream – a vision to unify and uplift the Bishnupriya Manipuri identity. In a world moving faster than ever, we needed a space where culture, creativity, and community could meet with technology and modern storytelling.

Our initiatives go beyond just content. From heritage documentation to youth empowerment projects, every step we take is toward a future where our people are proud, aware, and connected. This is more than a project – it’s a movement. And you’re part of it.

What You’ll Find in Imarthar.Core

  • Cultural revival through books, blogs, and videos
  • Workshops, meetups, and tech camps for youth
  • Creative campaigns like the T-shirt movement
  • Behind-the-scenes stories of community heroes
